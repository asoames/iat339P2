Branding Ideas - Newegg Canada

What does the company do?

Newegg is a tech-focused e-retailer based in North America. Newegg provides customers with a comprehensive catalog of the latest consumer electronics, entertainment, smart home and gaming products. As well, Newegg regularly provides customers with deals and promotions on a variety of products.

Who is the company to potential customers?

Newegg is North America's leading tech-focused e-retailer and is ranked as one of the best online shopping destinations consistently. Newegg is similar to online retailers such as Amazon and eBay, but with a focus on tech related products. Unlike Amazon, eBay, and most other online retailers, Newegg regularly provides customers with 3 daily super deals, daily deals, deals on featured products, monitor deals, and email subscriber only deals. Therefore, customers will always be able to find new deals on Newegg whenever they visit their website.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product?

Newegg strives to offer competitive pricing on its unmatched product catalog, while firmly committing to on-time order fulfillment. Newegg allows customers to make the best purchasing decisions by providing detailed product information, product tutorials, customer reviews, expert opinions, and the ability to network with the Newegg community's other members. As well, Newegg puts customer satisfaction as thir top priority, which is achieved through their superior service and there customer first philosophy. Therefore, customers not only get great deals and detailed product info and reviews, but also high quality customer support.

Where did this idea come from?

With the rise in the price of electronics, computers, and computer parts, it has become increasingly more expensive for the average consumer to buy products that perform well in today's standards. As well, online retailers and marketplaces are constantly competing with each other in order to provide reasonably priced tech and computer products, while still trying to make a profit. As one of the best tech-focused e-retailer in North America, Newegg has become an increasingly more popular destination for online tech shopping due to their competitive pricing and commitment to top priority customer satisfaction. As such, I feel that the current website (the Newegg Canada site) could be updated to provide users with a better online shopping experience.

Brand Identity

Fonts

To best suit the simplistic, minimalist feel, I want to use sans-serif fonts that are simple and easy to read. Also, I want to use a mixture of regular and thin font weights in order to create a hierarchy between the body and headings.

  • Headings are styled using the font Lato

  • Body text is styled using the font Open Sans

Color Palette

rgb(25, 25, 112) || hex: #191970

rgb(0, 121, 202) || hex: #0079CA

rgb(246, 246, 246) || hex: #F6F6F6

Art Direction

Similar to other other retailers and market places, I want the site to have a minimalist feel, focusing on the products themselves
